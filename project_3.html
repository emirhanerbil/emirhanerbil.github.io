About Project

- Most discord music bots can no longer play music or videos from youtube.

- I decided to do this to find a solution to this.

- I rented a server on GCP and I download the audio of the YouTube videos in the incoming link with a library and transmit the audio with ffmpeg.

- This link can even be a playlist. It understands that it is a playlist and adds other songs to the queue. Or you can add songs to the queue by continuously typing !play

- Thanks to GCP's download speed, videos play much faster than most bots

- Can also play shorts

- For the bot to work, !play works as a link or !play "query".

- Sometimes it is difficult to find the link of the song we want and give it to the bot. That's why I set it to work without a link.

- After typing !play, you enter the song or video name you want. Using the YouTube API, the bot sends the first 5 results on YouTube as a message on Discord and waits for you to enter the order of which one you want to choose.

- For example, if you type 3, the music in the 3rd result starts playing.

Python Youtube API GCP Discord Ffmpeg

Features

  • It's free

    You can add this bot to your server for free

  • Search your songs

    We show the 5 best results of the song you messaged using the Youtube API and wait for you to choose.

  • You can queue your songs

    If you want to skip the song, you can type !skip and go to the next song.Thanks to the queue structure

  • Instant play

    Since the host is on gcp, the songs play instantly, no wait.
